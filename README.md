Project Overview
This repository contains the source code and content for my professional portfolio website, designed to present my academic background, leadership experience, and research interests in economics.

The project serves as a centralized platform to showcase:

My academic journey as a second-year Economics student at Addis Ababa University

Leadership experience as a departmental student representative

Certified training and coursework (including World Bank–related online field work)

Research interests focused on Ethiopia’s and Africa’s major economic challenges

Purpose
The primary goal of this project is to provide universities, researchers, employers, and development institutions with a clear, professional overview of my profile, interests, and future direction.

This portfolio is built to:

Communicate credibility and seriousness

Highlight policy- and research-oriented economic interests

Demonstrate initiative, discipline, and long-term academic vision

Key Focus Areas
The portfolio emphasizes economic issues relevant to Ethiopia and Africa, including:

Inflation and macroeconomic stability

Unemployment and youth labor markets

Poverty and income inequality

Food security and agricultural development

Gender-based economic disparities

These themes reflect my commitment to evidence-based analysis and sustainable development goals.

Technologies Used
HTML5 – Structure and content

CSS3 – Layout and styling

(Future updates may include JavaScript and data visualization tools)

Current Status
This project is actively maintained and will be updated as I progress academically, complete new research, and gain additional certifications or experience.
